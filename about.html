Carnifex

ABOUT

Carnifex is a deathcore band from Fallbrook, CA. They were formed in 2005 by lead vocalist Scott Ian Lewis and Shawn Cameron. They have released 8 albums over their 20 year career 3 under Victory Records and 5 under Nuclear Blast Records. They did have some EPs released in the early 2000s with the release of their self-titled album. They later had Love Lies in Ashes in early 2007 before they began work on their first full length album.

This image was taken as PR for the release of the bands 9th album release Necromanteum.
